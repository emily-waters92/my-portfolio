Buddy




Buddy was created to help people coming from overseas, connect with locals in the Wellington region to share their culture, language and passions. It was the final group project that I was involved in at Dev Academy. My group wrote this project in Typescript using React, Auth0, Express, Node.js and SQL and we practiced agile methodologies throughout our time creating together. I learned a lot about the importance of planning and communication in this project. While they both do take up a fair amount of time, like they say "Fail to prepare, prepare to fail". Both planning and communication are the biggest lessons I took away from the project, they are both invaluable when it comes to effectively working in a team.
